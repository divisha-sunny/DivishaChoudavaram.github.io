NYC Trip Analysis Dashboard

The NYC Trip Analysis Dashboard is created using trip records data provided by the Taxi and Limousine Commission from the years 2016 and beyond, amounting to a total of 615 million trip records. This comprehensive dashboard offers an in-depth analysis of taxi trips in New York City, unveiling key insights that encompass the busiest pickup and drop-off locations, peak trip times, and fare variations throughout the day. It provides valuable insights into the traffic dynamics of NYC. Moreover, the dashboard conducts a thorough analysis of cab services, encompassing Yellow taxis, Green taxis, and ride-sharing platforms such as Uber, Lyft, Juno, and Via. These insights are particularly useful for those looking to venture into the New York City taxi industry.

Data Visualization and Analysis of At-Risk Youth Data

Collaborated with KARA, a non-profit organization, to analyze data on at-risk youth. Using Python, worked on cleaning non-granular, unstructured, and inconsistent data from 22 Excel worksheets. Additionally, utilized Tableau to create an interactive dashboard that provided visual representations of the analyzed data. The analysis of the data revealed specific areas with poor metrics related to child abuse and neglect. These findings were instrumental in supporting KARA's efforts in writing a book that focuses on child abuse and its impact on both children and the communities they live in.
