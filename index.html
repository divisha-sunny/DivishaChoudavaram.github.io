Data Visualization and Analysis of At-Risk Youth Data

Collaborated with KARA, a non-profit organization, to analyze data on at-risk youth. Using Python, worked on cleaning non-granular, unstructured, and inconsistent data from 22 Excel worksheets. Additionally, utilized Tableau to create an interactive dashboard that provided visual representations of the analyzed data. The analysis of the data revealed specific areas with poor metrics related to child abuse and neglect. These findings were instrumental in supporting KARA's efforts in writing a book that focuses on child abuse and its impact on both children and the communities they live in.
